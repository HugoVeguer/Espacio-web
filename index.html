My School Life

High School

The IES Clara Campoamor is a public secondary school located in Alaquàs, a town in the province of Valencia, Spain. The school offers a wide range of educational programs, including compulsory secondary education (ESO) and Bachillerato (high school), catering to students from diverse backgrounds. It is known for its commitment to academic excellence and fostering a positive learning environment.
The institution is named after Clara Campoamor, a prominent Spanish politician and feminist who played a crucial role in the women's suffrage movement in Spain. The school emphasizes the development of both academic and personal skills, promoting values such as respect, tolerance, and responsibility among its students.
In addition to its academic offerings,IES Clara Campoamor provides various extracurricular activities, such as sports, cultural events, and student associations, to enrich the students' overall experience. The school is equipped with modern facilities, including classrooms, a library, and sports areas, creating a conducive environment for learning and personal growth. It also encourages international collaboration and student exchange programs to enhance global awareness and cultural understanding.
